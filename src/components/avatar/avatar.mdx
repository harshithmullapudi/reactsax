## Default

## Group

You can group several avatar with the parent component `rs-avatar-group`, this component has two interesting properties:

float: causes the avatar components to be placed on each other and the grouping of one on top of the other is eliminated

<Playground>
  <section class="d-section">
    <div class="d-section__item">
      <AvatarGroup>
        <Avatar text="Rohith" />
        <Avatar text="Harshith" />
        <Avatar text="Rupesh" />
      </AvatarGroup>
    </div>
    <div class="d-section__item">
      <AvatarGroup float>
        <Avatar text="Rohith" />
        <Avatar text="Harshith" />
        <Avatar text="Rupesh" />
      </AvatarGroup>
    </div>
  </section>
</Playground>
